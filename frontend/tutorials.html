Squealing brake pads or rotors

High-pitched squeals often indicate worn pads or lack of lubrication.

Grinding gears during shifting

Could indicate worn synchros or clutch issues in manual transmissions.

Loose or rattling muffler

Usually caused by loose brackets or rusted components in the exhaust system.

Whistling sound from air intake

May indicate air filter blockage or vacuum leak.

Clicking sound from CV axle

Often heard when turning, indicating worn CV joints.

Buzzing from fuel pump

A continuous buzz can suggest a fuel delivery issue or clog.

Hissing from vacuum leak

This can disturb fuel-air ratios and trigger check engine lights.

Clunking in the suspension

Could indicate bad bushings or sway bar links.

Ticking sound from valve lifters

Common with low oil pressure or worn components in the top-end.
